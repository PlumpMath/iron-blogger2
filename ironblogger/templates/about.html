About Iron Blogger

Iron Blogger is a friendly little game people use to get themselves to blog more. The software that runs this website is free and open source; you can get the code here.

Rules: Summary

  • Post once a week.
  • If you don't turn in a post, you owe $5.
  • If you turn in a post late, you owe $1 for each week that it's late (max $5)
  • You can't owe more than $30.
  • We'll spend the money on parties for everyone! afterwards, all the accounting resets.

Pedantic Details

  • Each participant is expected to publish one blog post per week.
  • If a participant misses a post, they owe $5.00 for that post.
  • If a participant posts more than once in a given week, subsequent posts will reduce the debt incurred by any posts the participant had previously missed.
  • We will periodically have parties funded from the debt people incurr. After the party (and once debts have been paid), the accounting resets.
  • No participant shall owe more than $30.00 per party.

Regarding the last bullet point: When a participant publishes an extra post, it counts as the most recent post that the participant has missed. Rather than owing $5.00 for that post, the participant will owe $N, where the post is N weeks (rounded up) late, provided N is less than 5.
